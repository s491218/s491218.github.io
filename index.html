Computer Science Game Project

Our project is a remake of Frogger

A short description of the game

The object of the game is to get your player to the other side of the screen without getting hit. Cars on the road are a threat to take you out at any point.Once you have cleared the cars, you must use the constantly moving logs to make your way to the other side of the forest. But if you misstep then you will fall into the water.
